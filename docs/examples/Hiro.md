##### Alertness I 

*Keen Senses:* Add the lower of your ***Intelligence*** or ***Dexterity*** as a **Bonus** to **Awareness Rolls**.

##### Athlete I 

Whenever you roll an *Athletics* test with ***Strength***, you can also add your ***Agility*** as a **Bonus** to the roll. Whenever you roll an *Athletics* test with ***Agility***, you can also add your ***Strength*** as a **Bonus** to the roll. Your GM has final say if this might not be applicable in some rolls (e.g. a pure feat of ***Strength*** to lift a boulder).

##### Combat Reflexes I 

*First to Act:* If you roll no **Hitches** (or you reduce your **Hitches** to 0\) in an **Initiative** **Roll**, increase your **Initiative** by 1 (up to your **Max Initiative**).   
*Honed Reflexes:* Gain a d8 **Bonus** to any out of combat roll that has to do with reflexes and reaction time.  
**✪ Quick Draw** *(Requires **Dexterity** d8)*	  
*Quick Fingers:* Reduce the **Cost** of the *Quickdraw* **Effect** by 2\.   
*Reflexive Draw:* You may **Invoke** *Quickdraw* in a *Reflexive Attack* for the normal cost. 

##### Defense II 

*Guards:* If you *Parry*, reduce the **Cost** of the *Swift Recovery* **Effect** by 2\.   
*Wards:* Increase the **Value** of the **Die** you **Assign** to **Invoke** the *Block* **Effect** by 2 (before halving).   
**✪ Hard to Hit & ✪✪ Step Aside**  
*Combat crouch:* Any **Bonuses** to you or **Penalties** to enemies due to you being behind **Cover** are improved by one step, to a max of d12.  
*Crooked Strike:* If you *Parry*, gain your *Reflexes* as a **Bonus**.  
*Voiding:* If you *Dodge*, add as a **Bonus** your *Melee* if against a melee weapon, or your *Unarmed* if against an unarmed attack.   
*Zig-Zag:* As long as you have enough room to move around (GM’s discretion, a 2-3 meter radius all around is enough) and you don’t benefit from **Cover** (except from a shield), any attack against you suffers your ***Agility*** as a **Penalty**.

##### Fortitude I 

*Enduring:* You gain a d8 **Bonus Die** to **Stamina Rolls** involving exertion, physical labor or fatiguing situations, from farm work to forced marches, including recovering from **Fatigue** and **Exhaustion** including the *Second Wind* **Action**, but not rolls for **Incapacitation**.   
*Hardy:* You have one additional **Strain Slot**.   
**✪ Tough**  
*Ignore Pain:* Reduce the **Pain** **Penalty** from being **Injured** by one step.  
*Recover:* The **Bonus** from *Enduring* applies to **Stamina Rolls vs Incapacitation**.

##### Presence I

*Leadership:* When you are trying to convince, inspire, cajole or threaten your followers to a specific course of action (e.g. entering a scary dungeon), you gain a d8 **Bonus**.  
*Motivate:* Outside of combat, if you personally direct anyone willing to listen, they can add your ***Personality*** as a **Bonus** to one specific action. 

##### Resilience I 

*Discipline:* When you **Push** on a **Fear Roll**, **Self-Control Roll**, or other roll where your discipline would help you like against coercion or outright mind control, you can, instead of rerolling a **Die**, set it to half its maximum **Value** plus 1 (e.g. 5 if a d8).   
*Unperturbed:* Increase your **Composure Threshold** by 1\.  
**✪ Bravery**  
*I got this:* When making a **Fear Roll**, you may add any one ***Attribute*** or *Skill* as a **Bonus Die**, assuming it is relevant to the situation and the GM approves (e.g. *Athletics* against a fear of heights, *Melee* or *Unarmed* against a fearsome foe etc.). You cannot choose ***Willpower*** or *Resolve*. 

##### Survival II 

*Camouflage:* Gain a d10 **Bonus** when sneaking up on someone or hiding from them when in wilderness.  
*Survival Training: G*ain a d10 **Bonus** to any roll that your survival training would help- navigating the wilderness, foraging for food or tracking your quarry, as well as against environmental hazards like blizzards or desert storms.

##### Warrior II 

*Combat Sense:* When you perform a melee or unarmed attack against a target without the Warrior **Talent** or with Warrior I, you may choose to perform **Step 5B** (**Defender** **Assigns Dice for Effect**) first, then perform **Step 5A** (**Attacker** **Assigns Dice for Effect**).   
*Veteran:* When you **Push** yourself during a melee or unarmed *Attack* or a *Parry*, you can, instead of rerolling a **Die**, set it to half its maximum **Value** plus 2 (e.g. 6 if a d8).  
**✪ Grappling Expertise**  
*Armed Grappling:* If you attack a target you have **Grappled**, with an unarmed attack while holding a single weapon, you may add its **Accuracy** as a **Bonus**.     
*Holds:* Any target **Grappled** by you suffers your *Unarmed* as a **Penalty** in addition to your ***Strength***.  
**✪ Sword Expertise & Sword Mastery**  
*Fencing:* During an *Attack* or *Parry* with a sword, gain a **Bonus** equal to your *Melee* or the sword’s *Accuracy*, whichever is lower.   
*Half-Sword:* If you have one hand free or are wielding a sword in both hands, you may consider a sword’s **Reach** as two steps lower, and reduce the **Cost** of the *Weak Spot* **Effect** by 1 for attacks with the sword. You cannot use this benefit if you cannot somehow grab the blade of your sword safely (e.g. an energy blade).  
*Master-Strike:* When you *Attack* with a sword and **Invoke** *Block*, if the attack is successful, do not halve the **Value** of the **Assigned Die.**  
*Perfect Balance:* The **Accuracy** of any sword you are holding counts as one step higher; if it was already d12, then you ignore 1 **Hitch** in an *Attack* or *Parry* with it.
